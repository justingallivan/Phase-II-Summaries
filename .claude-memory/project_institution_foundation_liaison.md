**User-attested (S159, WMKF authority тАФ best current understanding, not probe-proven semantics).** Grantee institutions assign a **dedicated liaison to the Foundation who stewards research grants**; that person тАФ not the principal investigator тАФ is what `akoya_request.akoya_primarycontactid` (lookup тЖТ `contact`, label "Primary Contact") holds. **For large gifts, the contact routes through the institution's President's office.** Probe-substantiated *behavioral signature* corroborates the user's *semantic*: one liaison recurs across many unrelated grants for an institution (Hossein @ USC, Nelly @ Caltech тАФ ~8 and ~18 unrelated science topics respectively), and the institution President appears on big/institutional gifts (USC's Carol Folt, Caltech's Thomas Rosenbaum). **The contact-role triad (all user-attested S159, WMKF authority; behavioral-signature corroborated, `akoya-pi-fields-/projectleader-by-program-2026-05-17.txt`, #1002794 Wen Li / USC Khajavikhan-Wang / Caltech Cai-Prober-Jackson).** Three distinct person fields on `akoya_request` тАФ do not conflate:
- `akoya_primarycontactid` ("Primary Contact") = the **foundation liaison / grant steward** (President's office for large gifts) тАФ NOT the PI.
- `wmkf_projectleader` ("Project Leader") = the **PI / scientific lead** тАФ the actual researcher; varies with the science. **Program-conditional**: research-process near-complete (~90тАУ98% native: Medical Research 98%, Science & Engineering 90%, Bridge Funding 100%); non-research ~0% (no PI concept тАФ institutional/discretionary gifts). Whole-entity 16/32% mig/nat is a process-pooled fiction тАФ never cite it.
- `wmkf_researchleader` ("Research Leader") = the **institution's senior research officer** (e.g. a VP-for-Research / Provost; Wayne State's Ezemenari Obasi, USC's Ishwar Puri) тАФ NOT the PI.

**Why:** a populated, real-looking person-name column labeled "Primary Contact" is the *plausible-wrong-answer* trap тАФ an analyst will assume it's the researcher and mis-attribute (e.g. a genomics grant to a university President or a grants officer). The field is the institution's contact-of-record, a relationship/stewardship role, not scientific authorship.

**How to apply:** any surface exposing these (Power Tools bulk export [[project_dataverse_power_tools]], Grant Reporting auto-fill, contact enrichment, Reviewer Finder COI) must label `akoya_primarycontactid` as the foundation liaison / grant steward (President's office for large gifts) **never as the PI**, and surface `wmkf_projectleader` as the PI for research-scoped data (with `N/A тАФ no PI (non-research process)` where structurally absent, never blank). Fill is 70%/77% mig/nat globally but **migrated-era fill is institution-variable (~41тАУ79% observed)** тАФ render null as the not-captured sentinel, never blank.
